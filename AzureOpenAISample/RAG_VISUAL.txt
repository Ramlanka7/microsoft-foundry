Q: "What is RAG?"
A: "RAG combines document retrieval with LLM generation. We search
    for relevant documents, inject them as context, and the LLM
    generates factually grounded answers with source citations."

Q: "Why RAG over fine-tuning?"
A: "RAG is more cost-effective and flexible. Update knowledge by
    adding documents, not retraining. Also provides transparent
    sources. Combine both: fine-tune for style, RAG for facts."

Q: "How prevent hallucination?"
A: "Low temperature (0.3-0.5), explicit prompt instructions to use
    only provided context, quality retrieval, and post-processing."
